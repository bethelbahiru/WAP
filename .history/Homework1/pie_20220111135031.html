Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie

30 Min - Prep time

10 Min - Cook time

40 Min - Total

8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 2 tbsp all-purpose flour 3 tbsp cornstarch 1/4 tsp salt 1 1/2 cups water 2 lemons, juiced and zested 2 tbsp butter 4 egg yolks, beaten 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked 4 egg whites 6 tbsp white sugar
